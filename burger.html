Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Divide the ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  3. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  5. Top the patties with cheese slices, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and any other desired toppings.
  6. Spread condiments on the top half of the buns and place them on top of the assembled burgers.
  7. Secure the burgers with toothpicks if necessary, and serve hot.
