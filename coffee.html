Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Coffee beans
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Grind the coffee beans to your desired consistency.
  2. Boil water in a kettle.
  3. Place the coffee grounds in a coffee maker or French press.
  4. Pour the hot water over the coffee grounds and let it steep for a few minutes.
  5. Press the plunger down in the French press or start the coffee maker.
  6. If desired, add sugar and/or milk to taste.
  7. Pour the coffee into a mug and enjoy!
