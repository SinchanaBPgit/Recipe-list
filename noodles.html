Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, etc.)
  • Protein (chicken, shrimp, tofu, etc.)
  • Sauce (soy sauce, oyster sauce, etc.)
  • Oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  3. Add vegetables and protein to the pan and stir-fry until cooked.
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan.
  5. Pour in the sauce and toss everything together until well combined.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
