Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella, cheddar, etc.)
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Herbs and spices (oregano, basil, garlic powder, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce, followed by your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for the specified time, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with herbs and spices, slice, and serve hot.
