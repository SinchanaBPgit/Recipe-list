Tea Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Tea leaves or tea bags
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar or sweetener (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a pot or kettle.
  2. Add tea leaves or tea bags to a teapot or cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the tea leaves or tea bags.
  4. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on your desired strength.
  5. Remove the tea leaves or tea bags.
  6. Add milk and sugar or sweetener to taste, if desired.
  7. Stir well and enjoy!
